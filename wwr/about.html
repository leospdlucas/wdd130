White Water Rafting

White Water Rafting is a popular and affordable rafting experience that offers a unique and memorable experience for both beginners and experienced rafters alike. Our team of experienced guides and crew members will guide you safely and comfortably through the lush, picturesque waters of Lake Michigan.

History

White Water Rafting was founded in 2018 by a team of experienced guides and crew members who have been raftering for over 15 years. Our mission is to provide a safe and enjoyable rafting experience for everyone, regardless of their experience level.
